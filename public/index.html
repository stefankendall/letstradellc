Trade

We negotiate deals on craigslist, and hand off to you. You meet a seller at a time and place that works for you and make the trade.

You send us pictures of the item, and we list on eBay. Because we do bulk volume, we get discounts on fees.

Ship

You print out a label we email you, and drop your package in a blue USPS box anywhere.

Get paid

Once the package is shipped, we pay you the cost of the original item and 50% of the profit.

You can expect to make $20-$60 a trade.

We'll never pay you less than $20.

Protection

We deal with PayPal fraud and manage the returns process for you. You get paid and keep trading, even if the eBay buyer becomes a pain in the ass.

Earn

The best month for a buyer so far is $1000 profit. Average profit is around ~$500/month, but this will vary based on your market.
